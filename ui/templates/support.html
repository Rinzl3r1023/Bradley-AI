SUPPORT & FAQ

HOW DOES BRADLEY DETECT AI GENERATED CONTENT?

Bradley uses advanced AI models to analyze video content for signs of manipulation. We examine visual artifacts, temporal inconsistencies, and other markers that indicate AI-generated content. Our detection works in real-time as you browse.

WHAT DO THE DIFFERENT BADGES MEAN?

Human-Generated (Green): 70%+ confidence that content is authentic.

AI-Generated (Red): 70%+ confidence that content contains AI manipulation.

Unknown (Yellow): Below 70% confidence - result is inconclusive.

WHY IS MY RESULT "UNKNOWN"?

An "Unknown" result means our detection confidence is below 70%. This can happen with heavily compressed videos, unusual formats, very short clips, or subtle manipulation techniques. We believe in honest uncertainty - we won't claim high confidence when we don't have it.

DOES BRADLEY STORE MY VIDEOS?

No. Bradley only sends the video URL to our API for analysis. We do not download, store, or retain any video content. Detection results are stored locally in your browser only. See our Privacy Policy for details.

HOW MANY VIDEOS CAN I ANALYZE?

Free users on the website can analyze 3 videos per hour. Install our Chrome extension for unlimited automatic scanning of all videos you encounter while browsing.

WHICH PLATFORMS DOES BRADLEY WORK WITH?

The Chrome extension works on virtually any website with video content, including YouTube, Twitter/X, Facebook, news sites, and more. The URL analyzer supports direct video links and embeds from most major platforms.

IS BRADLEY 100% ACCURATE?

No AI system is perfect. Bradley uses state-of-the-art detection but may occasionally produce false positives or miss some deepfakes. That's why we show confidence scores and use "Unknown" when uncertain. Always use multiple sources when verifying important content.
