BRADLEYAI

PRIVACY POLICY

WHAT WE COLLECT

  • Public video URLs from websites you visit (for AI content analysis only)
  • Detection results (stored locally in your browser)
  • Anonymous usage statistics (scan counts, threat counts)

WHAT WE DON'T COLLECT

  • Browsing history
  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Passwords or sensitive data
  • Video content itself (only URLs are sent to API)

HOW WE USE DATA

  • Video URLs are sent to our secure API for AI content analysis
  • Results are stored locally in your browser (Chrome storage API)
  • Anonymous statistics help improve detection accuracy
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties

DATA SECURITY

  • All API communication uses HTTPS encryption
  • No data stored on our servers (stateless API)
  • Local storage only (controlled by you)

YOUR RIGHTS

  • Disable extension at any time (stops all scanning)
  • Clear threat log anytime (delete local data)
  • Uninstall extension (removes all local data)

CHANGES TO POLICY

We may update this policy as Bradley evolves. Check this page for updates.
