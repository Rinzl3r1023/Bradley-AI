# Bradley AI Messaging Guide

## Official Terminology

### Primary Language (Consumer-Facing)
- "AI-generated content" or "AI content"
- "AI content detection"
- "Video authenticity verification"
- "Human-created" vs "AI-generated"
- Avoid "Deepfake" in consumer-facing copy
- Avoid "Fake video" (imprecise)
- Avoid "Synthetic media" (too technical)

### Badge Labels
- Red Badge: "AI Generated" or "AI Content"
- Green Badge: "Human Created" or "Authentic"
- Yellow Badge: "Unknown" (honest uncertainty)

### Technical/Internal Language (Docs, API, Code)
- "Deepfake" acceptable in technical contexts
- Function names can retain "deepfake" (avoid breaking changes)
- API responses maintain compatibility

## Brand Voice
- Transparent, not alarmist
- Helpful, not fear-based
- Clear, not jargon-heavy
- Universal, not niche
