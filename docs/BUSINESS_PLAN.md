## I. Executive Summary

Bradley AI is positioned to become the global standard for AI content verification—the Norton AntiVirus for the deepfake era. This document outlines a three-phase strategy that solves the bootstrap problem, creates enterprise leverage, and ultimately delivers truth verification as a free public utility funded by Big Tech.

Bradley is not a 'deepfake detector' or 'AI detection tool.' **Bradley is third-party safety infrastructure**—an independent verification layer that platforms need but cannot build themselves due to trust dynamics. Just as Norton/McAfee became essential despite Windows having built-in security, Bradley will become essential because trust requires independence.

| Phase 2 | 2027-2028 | Enterprise Transition | $10M-$30M/year |

### Key Innovation

The breakthrough insight: Start with freemium (proving demand and generating immediate revenue), transition to enterprise (platforms pay for API + liability shield), then flip to free (Big Tech funds truth infrastructure as a public good). The journey from paid to free becomes the PR story itself.

## XIV. Token Economics ($BRADLEY)

### Supply Model

Bradley uses a fixed supply with buyback model—no inflation, no burn. This creates simplicity, predictability, and sustainability. Buybacks create the same scarcity effect as burns while maintaining liquidity and supporting node operator income.

### Token Allocation

| Category | Allocation | Tokens | Vesting |
|----------|------------|--------|---------|
| Genesis/Founder (Chris) | 10% | 100M | 4-year linear, 1-year cliff |
| Team (Kim, Bryan, advisors) | 5% | 50M | 4-year linear, 1-year cliff |
| Early Investors (if raising) | 5% | 50M | 2-year linear |
| Community Distribution | 30% | 300M | 2-3 year distribution |
| Node Rewards Pool | 30% | 300M | 10-20 year distribution |
| Bradley Foundation | 10% | 100M | No vesting (operational) |
| Bradley Labs Treasury | 10% | 100M | No vesting (operational) |

**Total Supply: 1 Billion $BRADLEY tokens (FIXED FOREVER)**

### Why No Burn?

- Buybacks create demand (same scarcity effect as burns)
- Recirculation sustains nodes (better for operator income)
- Preserves liquidity (healthier trading ecosystem)
- Simpler to explain (passes the 'Mom test')
- Mirrors Bitcoin model (fixed supply, proven)

## XVIII. Liability Protection & Insurance

### How Liability Actually Works

When a platform like Twitter contracts with Bradley, they contract with Bradley Labs (not Foundation, not nodes). Labs provides API access to the decentralized network and carries insurance.

### Platform Contract Structure

- Platform contracts with Bradley Labs (entity exists, can be sued)
- Labs provides API access to decentralized network
- Labs carries E&O insurance (platform named as additional insured)
- If verification fails → insurance claim (not lawsuit against platform)

### Labs' Legal Defense

- 'We provide access, network decides'
- 'Decentralized consensus, not our determination'
- Intermediary liability protection (Section 230 parallel)

### Insurance Phases

| Phase | Timeline | Approach | Cost |
|-------|----------|----------|------|
| Phase 1: Self-Insurance | 2026 | $5M cash reserves set aside | $5M (one-time) |
| Phase 2: Traditional E&O | 2027 | Insurance with proven accuracy data | $100K-$500K/year |
| Phase 3: Specialized Coverage | 2028+ | Competitive 'Decentralized AI Liability' market | Premiums decrease |

### Founder Protection

- **Corporate Veil:** Officers/directors of Labs & Foundation, corporate structure limits personal liability
- **D&O Insurance:** Directors & Officers insurance covers personal lawsuits
- **IP Assignment:** IP assigned to Foundation (not personally liable)
